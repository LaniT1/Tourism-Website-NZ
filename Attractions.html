Attractions

Some of New Zealands best Tourist Attractions

Explore Milford Sounds

Milford Sound is a fiord in the southwest of New Zealand’s South Island. Mitford sound was carved by glaciers and consists of dark waters surrounded with cliffs and mountains, with waterfalls cascading down. There are many ways to explore Milford Sound, with the most popular being a Milford Sound Cruise. These cruises can either be during the day or overnight both giving an excellent experience. On these cruises you will be able to get close up to the beautiful geography and wildlife. Other ways to explore Milford Sounds is by a range of adventure activities such as kayaking or multi day hikes. Another way is by scenic flights. During these flights you will be able to experience a birds eye view of the fiords, seeing the white mountain tops, gushing waterfalls and blue lakes.

Hukafalls Jet Boat

Speed down the river of Hukafalls, located in Taupo. The Huka Falls are a set of waterfalls on the Waikato River that drains into Lake Taupō and is one of New Zealands most visited natrual attractions. The jet boat is 30 minutes of twist and turns. Get close to the native bush, sheer rock cliff face and hot water streams. The boat goes at 80km per hour along with 360 degree spins. When reached Huka Falls you will have have a unique vantige point as you will be on the water. Hukafalls Jet is the only jet boat company operating on this stretch of the Waikato River and offers one of the best jet boat rides in New Zealand. This adrenaline ride will leave you smiling and leave your heart racing.

Bungy Jump or Luge down the mountain in Queenstown

Bungy jumping and Luging is two popular tourist activities in Queenstown. Queentown is home to many bungy jump locations, all offering a thrilling experience. If you want something for all ages, visit skyline in Queenstown and ride the Luge. On these gravity-fuelled carts you have full controling allowing you to go at your own pace. Additionally with these activities you will have spetacular views of the city or the beautiful south island nature

Hobbiton

If you want something less adventurous and are a fan of the Lord of the Rings and Hobbit trilogy take a tour of the movie set. Hobbiton is one of New Zealands popular family friendly tourist attraction, attraction 600,000 visitors per year. The Hobbiton film set is located in Matamata and offers tours and other activities. Hobbiton consists of 44 permanent structures, from hobbit houses to a Hobbit themed cafe where you can buy Hobbit themed beverages. When visiting you will be able to see where these movies were filmed and walk around becoming emerged in these worlds. You will also be able to experience New Zealands beautiful landscape and nature. There are multiple tour options to choose from, each with a wonderful experience, from breakfast tours to dinner/night tours.

Plus many more fun activities for all types of people!
