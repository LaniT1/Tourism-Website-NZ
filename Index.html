Tourism in New Zealand

New Zealand is an island country in the southwestern Pacific Ocean. It consist of two land masses and is home to a population of five million. New Zealand is known for its stunning landscape, from vast mountain ranges to beautiful coastline. Maori are the tangata whenua or indigenous people of New Zealand so while visiting learn, experience and embrace Maori traditions and culture.

All year round, New Zealand is a hot-spot for tourism offering a range of both winter and summer activities. Additionally New Zealand is a relatively easy country to navigate. Buses go everywhere, cars constantly pick up hitchhikers, campervans are easy to rent, and there are various bus tours around the country. Plus, there are trains and planes.

So whether your planning on visiting New Zealand or live in New Zealand, AroundNZ can help you. On this website you will be able to learn about New Zealands top tourist destinations and some of the best attractions this country has to offer.
