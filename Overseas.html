New York

New York is a city located in the United States of America. The city is made up of five boroughs, the Bronx, Brooklyn, Manhattan, Queens and Staten Island, all sitting where the Hudson River meets. In ways of tourist New York has two peak seasons: In the summer from June to August, and then again in winter from November to December. New York is known for many things such as its sky scrapers, Empire State building, Times square, Broadway and Statue of Liberty plus many more.

Things to do while in New York

Walk Central Park

Central Park is a park in New York City, located between the Upper West and Upper East Sides of Manhattan. Central Park covers 843 acres (843 Hectares)

Brooklyn Bridge

The Brooklyn Bridge is a hybrid cable-stayed/suspension bridge, connecting Brooklyn and Manhattan. Brooklyn Bridge was the first fixed crossing of the East River, opening in 1883.

See a Broadway show

Broadway is a cluster of famous theatres where vistors come to watch top musicals or plays. There are 41 theatres on Broadway and they are constantly hosting a range of changing live productions.

Explore Time square
